'Identity, Values and Strengths'

What is my long term goal or plan?

I’m not sure at all on a career pathway, but my main long-term goal is to find happiness and fulfilment in a balanced work-life, I hope to find an engaging and exciting career that will enhance it.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

I wish to work on my interpersonal skills, communication, focus and confidence.

A description of your own strengths and limitations

I think a lot of my strengths are under-developed and come with a lot of caveats: Cue all of those memes about how my weakness or limitations are actually my strengths,
Like Michael Scott.

I am very Curious and get interested in lots of different things, often picking up and getting interested in new things. However It means I can easily get distracted when things aren’t engaging, i need to learn to be more self-disciplined, to actually sit down and push through hard or uncomfortable work, and learning to apply myself to the material

While I like to consider myself a fast learner, and have had successes in the past, I don’t think it’s always the case, I sometimes struggle to really apply myself. I think I gave my self this fixed notion when I was younger to help with my self-esteem, and can now get quite frustrated (when I’m by myself) when I don’t know something or can’t understand it with little effort

I tend to reflect on myself a lot, but its always a very internal thing, something for myself with no desire to share. I will struggle with articulating it or communicating it to other people, and will expect to come up with very jumbled thoughts and reflection with loose connection to each other.

How you will manage your workload in this programme

I’m very used to learning with a depth of knowledge, trying to know everything I can, I will struggle to use a just-in-time learning approach. I have already found balancing this difficult during the start-up of my blog, where I thought I had an understanding of some of the learning competencies, and then got stuck following the rabbit to some curiosities that I wanted to implement into my blog, to come back and find that I hadn’t grasped the basics, and had to rush and change everything to not go overtime.
I think following the guidelines for time and learning competencies will be very important for me, Working to keep myself focused and find interest and meaning in the work to keep me engaged

How and where you will seek help in a timely way

After I have tried working out a problem by myself, from different angles struggling through it, I will then seek help from my facilitator and cohort when I am struggling to stay within time limits. I expect the facilitation team to help guide me through my journey with poignant feedback, and suggestions on where to spend more time if needed

Other commitments

While I will forgo most of my commitments and hobbies, I would like to continue to but cut back on exercise, I will try to climb or run once a day.
